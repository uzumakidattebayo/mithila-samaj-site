About Seva Foundation

Two decades of dedicated service to humanity

Our Mission

Seva Foundation stands as a beacon of hope for underprivileged communities across India. For over two decades, we have been committed to creating sustainable change through comprehensive development programs that focus on education, healthcare, women empowerment, and environmental conservation.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual, regardless of their socio-economic background, has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for personal growth. Our holistic approach ensures that communities are not just supported, but empowered to become self-reliant.

Our Services

Comprehensive programs designed to create lasting impact

Education & Literacy

Comprehensive education programs including primary schooling, adult literacy, vocational training, and digital literacy initiatives for rural and urban communities.

  • Primary & Secondary Education
  • Adult Literacy Programs
  • Vocational Training
  • Digital Literacy

Healthcare & Wellness

Mobile health clinics, preventive care programs, health awareness camps, and telemedicine services for remote areas.

  • Mobile Health Clinics
  • Preventive Care
  • Health Awareness

Women Empowerment

Skill development programs, microfinance initiatives, and leadership training to empower women economically and socially.

  • Skill Development
  • Microfinance Support
  • Leadership Training

Environment Conservation

Tree plantation drives, waste management programs, and renewable energy initiatives for sustainable development.

  • Tree Plantation
  • Waste Management
  • Renewable Energy
