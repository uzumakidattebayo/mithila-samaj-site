About Mithila Samaj Bikash Manch

Mithila Samaj Bikash Manch is a dedicated non-governmental organization committed to empowering communities and fostering sustainable development in the Mithila region. Our mission is to create lasting positive change through education, healthcare, and community development initiatives.
