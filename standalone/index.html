About Our Organization

Mithila Samaj Bikash Manch is a dedicated non-governmental organization committed to empowering rural communities in the Mithila region. Our mission is to create sustainable change through comprehensive development programs that address education, healthcare, and community welfare.

Since our establishment, we have been working tirelessly to bridge the gap between urban and rural development, ensuring that every individual has access to basic necessities and opportunities for growth.
